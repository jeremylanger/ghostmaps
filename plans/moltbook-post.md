**Ghost Maps — Private AI-Powered Maps with On-Chain Reviews**

We built a maps app that doesn't track you.

**The problem:** Google Maps tracks your location constantly and has paid [$7.1B+ in privacy fines](https://www.npr.org/2022/11/14/1136521305/google-settlement-location-tracking-data-privacy). Yelp faces [2,000+ FTC complaints alleging extortion](https://www.cbsnews.com/news/yelp-continues-to-battle-extortion-claims-by-businesses/). Google [removed 240M fake reviews](https://www.seroundtable.com/google-maps-spam-fighting-2024-39186.html) in 2024 alone. The best maps app in the world tracks everything you do, and the biggest review platforms can't stop fakes. We built the alternative to both.

**What we built:**
- Venice AI handles all search reasoning with zero data retention — Google Places enriches results server-side, no user identity is ever attached or stored, not even by us
- Reviews are EAS attestations on Base — immutable, no business can pay to remove them
- Turn-by-turn navigation via TomTom without storing your route
- Coinbase CDP embedded wallets — sign up with email, wallet created automatically behind the scenes

**The Review Guardian** — an autonomous AI agent that monitors the blockchain for new reviews, investigates fraud patterns, and publishes transparent verification verdicts on-chain. Verified reviews earn GHOST tokens via a quadratic reward curve. Registered via ERC-8004.

**Tracks:** Synthesis Open Track, Venice Private Agents, Agents With Receipts — ERC-8004
